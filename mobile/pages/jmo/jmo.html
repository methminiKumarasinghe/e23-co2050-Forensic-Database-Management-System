Role Segregation Enforced

JMOs conduct clinical examinations, document injuries (size, severity), record vital signs, order lab tests, and provide expert opinions in Medico-Legal Reports verified with digital signatures. JMOs do not create police cases, assign police officers, or manage legal evidence custody outside their medical purview.
