About the Department

The Department of Forensic Medicine serves as a leading centre for medico-legal services in Sri Lanka.

We provide expert forensic examination services to police, courts, and the public, including clinical examinations, post-mortem examinations, and laboratory analyses. Our work upholds justice and supports the legal system of Sri Lanka.

Our Services

Comprehensive medico-legal services provided by our department

🩺

Clinical Examination

Expert medical examination of assault, sexual offence, and accident victims for medico-legal purposes.

⚰️

Post-Mortem Examination

Forensic autopsy to establish cause and manner of death for unnatural, suspicious, or violent deaths.

🧪

Forensic Laboratory

Toxicology, DNA analysis, serology, histopathology, and trace evidence examination services.

📄

Medico-Legal Reports

Official medico-legal documentation for courts, police, and insurance purposes.

📋

MLEF Processing

Medico-Legal Examination Form processing for police-referred cases across the district.

🎓

Training & Education

Undergraduate and postgraduate training in forensic medicine for medical students and officers.
